## Work Prompt Pattern

```text

```

## Review

- Verify citations and permissions.
- Confirm names, dates, calculations and commitments.
- Distinguish generated suggestions from organizational facts.
- Review content before sending, publishing or updating records.
- Follow organizational policy for sensitive data and external sharing.
