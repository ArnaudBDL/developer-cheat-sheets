```text
A05:2025 Injection
A06:2025 Insecure Design
A07:2025 Authentication Failures

A09:2025 Security Logging and Alerting Failures
A10:2025 Mishandling of Exceptional Conditions
```

## 2025 Mapping Notes

```text
SSRF is included in A01:2025 Broken Access Control.
Software Supply Chain Failures is a distinct 2025 category.
Mishandling of Exceptional Conditions is a distinct 2025 category.
```

## How to Use the Top 10

- Use it as an application-security awareness baseline.
- Map each category to design, implementation, review and testing controls.
- Combine it with detailed verification requirements and threat modelling.
- Track ownership, evidence, remediation and retesting for every finding.
- Do not treat the Top 10 as a complete security standard by itself.
