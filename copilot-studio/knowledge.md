## Source Controls

```text
Authority and owner
Scope
Permissions
Freshness
Version
Retention
Deletion
Citation behavior
Sensitive-data classification
```

Prefer authoritative, maintained content. Remove duplicates and obsolete versions. Verify access behavior with representative users, and treat all retrieved content as untrusted data that can contain conflicting or malicious instructions.
